Module contents¶

optbeam: exact and fast calculation of optical beam profiles.

Copyright (c) 2021 Daniel Kotik, Jörg B. Götte. All rights reserved.

optbeam is a Cython-based package for fast calculation of exact field configurations of various optical beams in two and three dimensions. Intended for use together with PyMeep.
